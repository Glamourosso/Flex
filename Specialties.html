Our Specialties

Whether a single location, or a multi-location group with hundreds of locations, FlexScanMD’s inventory management provides you with real-time insight into your inventory needs. Features include:

  • Low on-hand and expiration date alerts
  • Real time inventory transfers between locations
  • Comprehensive reporting

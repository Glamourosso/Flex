Company Profile

Kestrel Software LLC was founded in Brooklyn, New York, in 2009. The company started as a general software development and consulting firm that designed custom software for various companies in diverse industries. In 2011 Kestrel Software LLC developed a web-based inventory tracking software system for a large hospital in the mid-west and gradually started selling a modified version of this software to other medical facilities.

Today Kestrel Software LLC’s primary software is FlexScanMD, the medical industry’s premier cloud-based inventory management and tracking system. Kestrel Software LLC has secured partnerships with some of the largest Practice Management and Electronic Health Record vendors in the country to provide them with FlexScanMD as an add-on inventory tracking module to their systems. Currently, FlexScanMD is used by thousands of medical practices worldwide covering almost every medical specialty.
